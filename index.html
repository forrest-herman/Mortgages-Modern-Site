About Me

Goals Goals Goals!


I love helping my clients reach their goals. Whether it’s purchasing your first home, refinancing your current home to pay off high-interest debt or buy a rental property and everything in between, I will coach you through the process from preapproval to closing and beyond.

When I’m not “running numbers” or meeting clients to set and achieve goals, I am a Mama to a high-energy little girl. I am a self-professed amateur foodie and a big supporter of local businesses. On my off time, you can find me exploring our beautiful city, enjoying the outdoors and networking with fellow “Big Dreamers”.

I am a proud agent with Today’s Mortgage Choice (Lic # 13324)- an Independent Brokerage with over 50 years of combined experience that allows me to better serve my clients and their mortgage needs.
